Haldi Ceremony

Blessings in golden hues

12:30 PM onwards

A sacred pre-wedding ritual where turmeric paste is lovingly applied to the bride and groom. This ceremony symbolizes purification and is believed to bring a natural glow to the couple.

Dress Code: Yellow or traditional attire

Sangeet Night

An evening of dance, music & celebration

6:30 PM onwards

A vibrant evening filled with dance performances, music, and celebration. Family and friends come together to perform choreographed dances and enjoy an unforgettable night.

Dress Code: Colorful festive wear

The Barat

The grand groom's procession

4:00 PM

The groom arrives in a magnificent procession accompanied by family and friends dancing to the beats of dhol. A joyous celebration marking the groom's journey to meet his bride.
